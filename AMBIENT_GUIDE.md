# 🌌 Ambient Atmosphere Guide

Your journal now includes beautiful ambient effects that enhance the emotional experience!

## ✨ **Features Added**

### **1. Visual Atmosphere**
- **Floating Particles**: Gentle floating orbs that drift across the screen
- **Gradient Overlays**: Mood-based color gradients that change with your emotional state
- **Light Rays**: Subtle animated light beams for depth
- **Vignette Effect**: Soft edges that create intimacy and focus
- **Noise Texture**: Subtle film grain for vintage feel

### **2. Mood-Based Effects**
The atmosphere responds to your current mood filter:

- **😊 Happy**: Warm yellow/orange gradients
- **🌙 Tired**: Cool blue/purple tones  
- **💭 Overthinking**: Neutral gray/slate colors
- **🤷‍♂️ Missing**: Soft pink/rose hues
- **🍃 Calm**: Peaceful green/teal shades

### **3. Ambient Sounds**
- **Keyboard Shortcut**: `Ctrl+A` to toggle ambient sounds
- **Volume Control**: Adjustable volume slider
- **Chord Generation**: Creates harmonic ambient tones
- **Visual Indicator**: Pulsing button when active

### **4. Settings Integration**
Ambient modes available in settings:

- **None**: Clean, minimal interface
- **Particles**: Floating orbs and particles only
- **Mood**: Color gradients based on current mood filter
- **Full**: Complete atmospheric experience
- **Rain**: Animated rain effect (coming soon)
- **Night**: Dark, moody atmosphere (coming soon)
- **Silence**: Minimal effects (coming soon)

## 🎮 **How to Use**

### **Enable Ambient Effects:**
1. Go to Settings (gear icon in sidebar)
2. Choose your preferred ambient mode
3. Effects apply immediately

### **Control Ambient Sounds:**
1. Press `Ctrl+A` to toggle sounds on/off
2. Click the volume slider to adjust
3. Visual indicator shows when active

### **Mood-Based Atmosphere:**
1. Filter notes by mood using sidebar
2. Watch the background change colors
3. Each mood creates unique emotional ambiance
